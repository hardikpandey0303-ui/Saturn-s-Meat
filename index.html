Founder & Leader

Reiko Onigahara

The leader and mastermind behind the operation of Saturn's Meat. Reiko started this restaurant through a fundraiser from her connections, with Yami soon joining her, the restaurant grew quickly to success.

Head Chef

Yami Chihara

Our head chef with over a decade of experience in cooking for fine dining restaurants, Yami brings out the true essence of the Japanese cuisine and his own secret recipes elevate them further to an otherworldly level, often quoted as the pride and heart of Saturn's Meat, Yami's food will have you licking your fingers and craving for more.

Lead Service

Eve Hoshina

Our leading service person for waiters, Eve has years of experience at Saturn's Meat and understands the true essence of Japanese fine dining. Customers will feel welcomed by her, as her experience will make sure that they have a fun and comfortable time in this area.

Catering

Nicholas

With his bright skills at catering, Nicholas here makes sure that your order reaches you within time. With him being fluent in German, French, Spanish, English and Japanese, Nicholas tends to be quick at understanding the needs of the tourists trying out Japanese cuisine for the first time.

Waiter

Charlotte Grove

Welcoming our newest crew member, Charlotte is from London, Britain. At just 21 years of age, Charlotte already has shown great potential in the fine dining industry and has prior experience at working in restaurants in Britain. A bright and cheerful girl who will make sure that you feel welcome right here in Saturn's Meat.
